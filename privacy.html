Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 June 2026

What I collect

When you contact Website Builder Nish through the website form, email, phone, or direct message, I may collect your name, email address, phone number, business details, and any information you include in your enquiry.

How I use your information

I use your information to reply to enquiries, prepare quotes, discuss projects, deliver services, and improve how I communicate with potential clients.

Forms and third-party services

This website uses Web3Forms to process contact form submissions. Information you submit through the contact form is sent securely through that service so I can receive and respond to your enquiry.

Advertising and analytics

If I run advertising campaigns, I may use website traffic data, referral information, and platform reporting to understand which ads are performing best. If analytics or tracking tools are added later, this policy should be updated to reflect them.

Data sharing

I do not sell your personal information. I may share information only with service providers that help me operate the business, such as hosting, form processing, email, or analytics providers, where reasonably necessary.

Data retention

I keep enquiry and client information for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to enquiries, manage projects, meet legal obligations, and maintain business records.

If you have questions about this policy or want to contact me about your information, email upnishadd@gmail.com.
